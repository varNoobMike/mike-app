About

This page is about myself, who is, undeniably, an exceptionally unique individual — not because of any particularly remarkable skills, but because I have mastered the fine art of convincing people that 'winging it' is a life philosophy. I am the proud owner of a chaotic schedule, an ever-growing collection of half-read books, and an extensive inventory of forgotten hobbies.

I may not be a Nobel Prize winner, but I am exceptionally good at surviving coffee-fueled all-nighters, and I consider it a personal achievement that I can stare at a screen for hours without losing my sanity (mostly).

When I'm not busy forgetting my to-do list, I’m passionate about *appearing* productive. I thrive on procrastination, live for the thrill of last-minute projects, and embrace the philosophy that anything worth doing is worth doing... eventually.

So if you’re here expecting life-changing wisdom, you’ll have to settle for my highly refined ability to laugh at my own misadventures. Feel free to join me in this journey of *almost-greatness*, as I try to navigate adulthood one cup of coffee at a time.
